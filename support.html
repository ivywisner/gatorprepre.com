Frequently Asked Questions

Is GatorPrep RE free to use?
Yes! GatorPrep RE is free to download and includes 3 quiz sessions, 1 practice exam, and all flashcards at no cost. Pro and Premium upgrades unlock unlimited access and are one-time purchases — no subscriptions required for Pro.
How do I restore my previous purchase?
Open the app and go to the paywall screen (tap any locked feature). Scroll to the bottom and tap "Restore previous purchase." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
Is this app specific to Florida?
Yes — GatorPrep RE is built exclusively for the Florida real estate sales associate exam. Every question is Florida-specific and covers the topics tested by FREC. It is not a national exam prep app.
What does Premium include that Pro doesn't?
Premium adds the full timed simulation — 100 questions with a 3 hour 30 minute countdown timer, replicating real exam conditions. Pro includes everything else: unlimited quizzes, unlimited practice exams, review missed questions, and exam history.
What is the Premium Weekly subscription?
Premium is available as a $21.99 one-time purchase or a $5.99/week subscription. The subscription auto-renews weekly and can be cancelled anytime in your iPhone's App Store settings under Subscriptions.
How accurate are the practice questions?
All 1,900+ questions are written specifically for the Florida real estate sales associate exam and cover the same topic areas and question types you'll see on test day.
Is GatorPrep RE available on Android?
GatorPrep RE is currently iOS only. An Android version may be available in the future.
