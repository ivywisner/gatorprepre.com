FAQ

Common Questions About the FL Real Estate Exam

How many questions are on the Florida real estate exam?
The Florida real estate sales associate exam consists of 100 multiple-choice questions. You'll have 3 hours and 30 minutes to complete it.
What score do you need to pass the Florida real estate sales associate exam?
You need a score of 75% or higher to pass — that's 75 out of 100 questions correct.
How hard is the Florida real estate exam?
The Florida real estate exam trips up a lot of first-time takers, particularly on the math and the license law sections. Focused, Florida-specific preparation makes a significant difference — generic national study tools won't cut it.
What topics are covered on the Florida real estate exam?
The Florida real estate sales associate exam covers topics including license law, brokerage relationships, real estate contracts, property ownership, mortgages, closing, valuation, and Florida & federal fair housing laws. GatorPrep RE covers all of these areas with questions designed around what FREC actually tests.
How many times can you retake the Florida real estate exam?
You can retake the Florida real estate exam as many times as needed within two years of completing your pre-license course. After two years you'll need to retake the course. Retakes are scheduled through Pearson VUE.
How many practice questions should I do before the Florida real estate exam?
There's no magic number, but the goal is consistent exposure across all topic areas until you're scoring confidently — not just memorizing answers. GatorPrep RE tracks your accuracy by topic so you always know which areas still need work before exam day.
What accuracy score should I aim for before taking the FL real estate exam?
We recommend aiming for 85% or higher across all topic areas before scheduling your exam. The actual passing threshold is 75%, but a comfortable buffer matters — especially since nerves are real on test day.
Is GatorPrep RE specific to Florida?
Yes — GatorPrep RE is built exclusively for the Florida real estate sales associate exam. Every question is Florida-specific and covers the topics tested by FREC. This is not a generic national app with Florida content tacked on.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — GatorPrep RE is free to download. The free tier includes 3 quiz sessions, 1 practice exam, and all flashcards. No credit card or email required.
